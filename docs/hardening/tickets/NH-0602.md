## Problem

The temporal campaign fixed sub-second version collapse (millisecond triggers) and AsOf plan-cache
rebinding, and the reconstruction fuzzer replays random add/update/delete chains through AsOf at
checkpoints BETWEEN versions. What the fuzzer deliberately does not reach: the EXACT-boundary AsOf
contract (a timestamp equal to a version's start - inclusive or exclusive is unpinned), history-row
fidelity over BOUNDARY values (it uses small ints/strings), and the interplay of a migration
recreate with a temporal table's history and triggers. This matrix closes those cells with the
established differential method.
